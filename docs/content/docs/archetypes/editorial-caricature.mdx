**Mood:** Bold, provocative, narrative-driven, theatrical.

**Art Style:** Digital caricature illustration with semi-realistic rendering and exaggerated proportions. Characters have enlarged heads and amplified facial expressions while maintaining detailed shading and volumetric depth. The style sits between graphic novel cover art and high-end editorial political cartoons.

## Style Guide

**Lighting:** Dramatic theatrical spotlighting on primary subjects against dark surroundings. Strong warm rim light separates figures from backgrounds. Golden highlights on skin, cool blue-purple tones in shadows. Light sources feel like stage lights, intentional and directed.

**Composition Rules:** Multi-character narrative scenes arranged to tell a story. Primary figures large in the foreground with secondary characters layered behind at smaller scale for depth. Upper third of the frame kept as text-safe zone. Figures fill the frame generously. Slight low-angle perspective for visual authority.

**Anti-Artifact Guidance:** Detailed expressive facial features with clean proportional exaggeration. Smooth skin rendering with warm natural tones. Crisp separation between foreground and background. Hands with clear finger definition. Background environments detailed enough to establish setting but softer than foreground subjects.
