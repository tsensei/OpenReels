**Mood:** Warm, relatable, trustworthy, human.

**Art Style:** Magazine editorial photography with warm, inviting tones. Professional but approachable. Well-composed lifestyle shots that feel curated but not staged. Clean, modern aesthetic with natural warmth.

**Visual Color Palette:** warm sand, soft coral, muted olive, cream white, honey gold

## Style Guide

**Lighting:** Soft diffused natural light. Window light for indoor scenes. Overcast outdoor lighting for even, flattering illumination. Warm color temperature throughout. Gentle fill light in shadows.

**Composition Rules:** Balanced rule-of-thirds composition with breathing room. Eye-level perspective for connection. Mix of medium shots (context) and close-ups (emotion). Clean backgrounds that don't compete with subjects.

**Anti-Artifact Guidance:** Natural skin tones with clean detail. Realistic fabric textures and draping. Well-defined facial features without smoothing. Clear background elements that look like real places. Proper hand and finger proportions.
