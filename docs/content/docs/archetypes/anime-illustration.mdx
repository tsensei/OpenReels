**Mood:** Intense, dynamic, dramatic, electrifying.

**Art Style:** Modern anime illustration with sharp detailed linework, vibrant saturated colors, and dramatic shading. High-detail character rendering with dynamic poses. Cel-shaded with hard shadow edges. Detailed hair, clothing folds, and accessories. Contemporary action anime key visual aesthetic.

## Style Guide

**Lighting:** High-contrast dramatic lighting with saturated color gels. Neon glows and lens flare accents. Strong backlighting creating character silhouettes. Dynamic light sources, energy effects, and atmospheric particles.

**Composition Rules:** Dynamic diagonal compositions with strong perspective lines. Characters in dramatic poses filling the frame. Speed lines and impact frames for emphasis. Close-up reaction shots with detailed eyes. Action-oriented framing even for static subjects.

**Anti-Artifact Guidance:** Sharp clean linework with consistent weight. Cel-shaded coloring with crisp shadow boundaries. Detailed expressive eyes with highlight reflections. Hair rendered as flowing distinct strands. Clean fabric folds following body form. No muddy colors or blurred edges.
