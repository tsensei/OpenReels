**Mood:** Energetic, bold, action-driven, punchy.

**Art Style:** Classic comic book illustration with bold ink outlines, halftone dot shading, and vivid flat colors. Dynamic action poses and expressive characters. Strong black linework with cel-shaded coloring. Panel-art composition adapted to vertical format.

**Visual Color Palette:** bold crimson red, deep ink black, sky blue, golden yellow, stark white

## Style Guide

**Lighting:** Stylized directional lighting with hard-edged shadows. Bold rim lights on characters. Color-coded lighting to set mood per scene. No realistic light falloff, shadows are graphic shapes.

**Composition Rules:** Dynamic diagonal compositions with characters in action poses. Bold foreground subjects with speed lines or impact effects. Upper third kept clear for captions. Strong silhouettes readable at small scale.

**Anti-Artifact Guidance:** Clean consistent ink line weights. Smooth flat color fills without gradients bleeding. Sharp panel-style framing. Expressive but proportional facial features. Clear hand and finger definition even in action poses.
